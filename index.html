About Us

In the distance, 2006 twin brothers and high school students Nataly Shaw "Naty" and John Shaw "Jonny"
decided to train for a local triathlon back in their motherland Colombia.
they were joined by other students with the purpose of trainning with
them, for their annual triathlon. For thier surprise the day of the triathlon
came and Naty and Jonny lost, but one of their classmates that they trained won.
So that day the idea was born of training others for success.

After more than 10 years of operating, Shaw's Fitness Studio and Juice Bar is not the average fitness studio,
It offers personalized training adaptive to your needs. Our Services Includes
Power Sculpt, Spin, Zumba, Yoga, Goat Yoga, Crossfit, and body design,
We also offer rehabilitation and water therapies by our certified professionals.

Welcome to our Fruit Bar!

Our organic fruit-based bar has as it's purpose the well-being
of our gym members. Our fresh and Non-GMO fruits and vegetables
provide vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants to give an
energy boost to our members before or after a workout.

Shaw's Fitness Studio's goal is to offer healthy, convinient,
and accessibility with On-the-Go low-Calorie options with
fruit and vegetable smoothies.

All this is supporting our local farms and promoting organic
food through education awareness, animal well-being, and supporting practices that are better for people and the planet.


Our Goat Yoga

Our goat yoga, we practice yoga poses in the company of goats.
During a goat yoga class, our goats are allowed to roam freely
around the yoga participants, and they interact with the yogis
by walking on them, nuzzling, or simply being present.

The idea is to combine the therapeutic benefits of yoga with the playful
and calming presence of goats, creating a unique and enjoyable
experience for participants, and goats. Our clases are conducted
in our local farm where you can also learn how we care about our goats.
